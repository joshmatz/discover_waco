Project 1: Waco Attractions Site

Description

Time to put your web design knowledge to work. This site will be a basic information site that would give someone information about local attractions.

Specifics

  • 2 or 3 column, fixed layout
  • Required header, vertical or horizontal navigation, main content area, footer
  • HTML and CSS must be commented for organization
  • Links
  • Images
  • List
  • Table

Objectives

  1. Demonstrate understanding of the Dreamweaver interface.
  2. Demonstrate understanding of the foundations of CSS and HTML.
  3. Demonstrate an understanding of site organization.
  4. Continue to demonstrate your understanding of design principles and elements.
  5. Demonstrate an understanding of following directions.
  6. Demonstrate skill of design and craftsmanship.

Have a weekend?

Whether you drive, fly, or walk to Waco, you're guaranteed a fun visit in the heartland of Texas. Situated on the I-35 corridor between Dallas and Austin, Waco's the place to stop for scores of shops and restaurants galore. So, come on by and stay for a visit. You'll find everything you need on the website to make your trip a success.
