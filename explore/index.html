Hit the trails

Waco has a variety of locations to immerse yourself if you're interested in history, nature or education. From a zoo that is home to 1,731 animals to the largest Baptist university that happens to currently be a powerhouse in all sports, there's something to find for everyone.

This list is only a brief look at what's available. For full listings, please view the Waco Convention and Visitors Bureau listings.

Have a weekend?

Whether you drive, fly, or walk to Waco, you're guaranteed a fun visit in the heartland of Texas. Situated on the I-35 corridor between Dallas and Austin, Waco's the place to stop for scores of shops and restaurants galore. So, come on by and stay for a visit. You'll find everything you need on the website to make your trip a success.
